History of the Bands

Learn a little more

1. The Breakout (1950s-1960s): Rock emerged from blues and country. Elvis kickstarted the movement, and The Beatles turned it into a global phenomenon, transforming the sound of guitars into the language of youth.

2. The Era of the Giants (1970s-1980s): The sound becomes heavy and massive. Stadium bands like Led Zeppelin and Queen emerge, while punk rises to remind us that anyone can start a band with just three chords.

3. Nirvana, rock becomes raw and personal. Today, the genre survives beyond trends, remaining the eternal symbol of rebellion and freedom. Translated with DeepL.com (free version)
